About The Owner

Hi, I am Matilda and I am the owner of Tillys Macaron.

I started this business a few months after I discovered the wonderful world of Macarons. I had come across a video tutorial on how to make them and decided to make them for my son's first birthday.
Oh! boy, did I fall in love with them. The way they melted in the mouth, and sweet goodness that filled my heart after each bite was wonderful.

I decided it would be a waste to not share this with others. Of course, making these delicious treats were tough but with lots of practice and encouragement from family and friends, I mastered these finicky goodness and decided with was time to share it with the world.

This is how Tilly's Macarons came to be.
